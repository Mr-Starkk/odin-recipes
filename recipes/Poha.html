What is it?

Poha is a popular Indian breakfast dish made from flattened rice. It's light, fluffy, and often flavored with turmeric, mustard seeds, and curry leaves. Additional ingredients can include onions, peas, and potatoes, garnished with fresh coriander and a squeeze of lemon. Poha is loved for its simplicity and quick preparation time.

Ingredients :

  1. 2 cupsPoha (flattened rice)
  2. 2 tablespoonsVegetable Oil
  3. 1 teaspoonMustard Seeds
  4. 10-12Curry Leaves
  5. 1Onion, finely chopped
  6. 1/2 cupPeas
  7. 1Potato, diced
  8. 1/2 tablespoonTurmeric Powder
  9. Salt, to taste
  10. 2 tablespoonsLemon Juice
  11. 2 tablespoonsFresh Coriander, chopped

Steps to prepare :

  1. Rinse the poha in cold water until it softens. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat the oil in a pan. Add the mustard seeds and curry leaves, and let them sizzle.
  3. Add the chopped onion and sautÃ© until it turns translucent.
  4. Add the peas and diced potato, and cook until they are soft.
  5. Stir in the turmeric powder and salt.
  6. Add the softened poha to the pan and mix well, ensuring the spices are evenly distributed.
  7. Cook for a few more minutes, then turn off the heat.
  8. Drizzle the lemon juice over the poha and mix well.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander before serving.
